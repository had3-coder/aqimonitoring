Safety Warnings

Important safety information for proper device operation

Device Handling & Precautions

  • Avoid Water & Extreme Conditions: The device is not waterproof; avoid exposure to rain, direct water splashes, or high humidity areas.
  • Proper Placement: Keep the device away from direct sunlight, heat sources, and high airflow areas to prevent inaccurate readings.
  • Safe Power Management: Ensure proper wiring connections to prevent short circuits. Do not exceed voltage limits (6V for solar input, 3.7V for battery).
  • Avoid Tampering: Unauthorized modifications may cause damage or data inaccuracy.

Emergency Procedures

  • Sensor Malfunction: Restart the device and recalibrate sensors. If the issue persists, replace the faulty sensor.
  • Fire or Overheating Risk: Immediately disconnect from power, move to a fire-safe location, and contact support.
  • Data Transmission Failure: Check Wi-Fi/GSM status, verify SIM card balance, and restart the device.

Disposal & Environmental Safety

  • Do not dispose of in regular waste: Batteries and electronic components should be taken to designated e-waste recycling centers.
  • Recycle responsibly: If replacing a sensor or battery, ensure proper disposal.
