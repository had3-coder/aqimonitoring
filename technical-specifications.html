Technical Specifications

Detailed technical information about the AQI monitoring system

Device Overview

  • Product Name: Cloud-Based Air Quality Monitoring System
  • Model: AQI-Monitor v1.0
  • Power Source: Solar-powered with LiPo battery backup
  • Connectivity: Wi-Fi & GSM for real-time data transmission

Measured Air Quality Parameters

Parameter Sensor Used Measurement Range
PM1, PM2.5, PM10 PMS7003 0 – 1000 µg/m³
CO (Carbon Monoxide) MiCS-6814 1 – 1000 ppm
NO₂ (Nitrogen Dioxide) MiCS-6814 50 – 10000 ppb
Temperature SHT41 -40°C to 125°C
Humidity SHT41 0 – 100% RH

Power & Battery Specifications

  • Primary Power Source: 6V 15W Solar Panel
  • Battery Backup: 3.7V LiPo Battery
  • Power Consumption: ~200mA during transmission, ~80mA in idle mode

Connectivity & Data Transmission

  • Wi-Fi: ESP8266 (2.4 GHz) for real-time cloud updates
  • GSM Module: SIM800L for SMS alerts & backup transmission
